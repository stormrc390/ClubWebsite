What we do

Camping

We like to spend time outdoors away from all busyness of modern life. Spending time outside with just primitive tools allows us to relax and enjoy the company of others.

Shooting

We hold compitition shooting events with primitive weapons. We practice shooting in-period weapons in competion for sport during the events.

Training

We hold training classes to teach children and young adults the primitive ways to do things to help them disconnect for technology and remember the way things were done in the past.
